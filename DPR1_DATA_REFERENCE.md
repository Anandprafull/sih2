## Project Overview

- **Project Name**: Integrated Tourism Development Project - Arunachal Pradesh
- **Objective**: Development of eco-tourism infrastructure and sustainable livelihood opportunities in the Eastern Himalayan region, focusing on community participation and environmental conservation
- **Total Budget**: ₹12.5 Crores (₹125,000,000)
- **Duration**: 24 months (01-04-2026 to 31-03-2028)
- **Location**: Tawang & Bomdila Districts, Arunachal Pradesh
- **Beneficiaries**: ~15,000 direct and 45,000 indirect beneficiaries

## Stakeholders

- **Client**: Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER)
- **Proposer**: Northeast Infrastructure & Development Consortium Pvt. Ltd.
- **GSTIN**: 12AABCT5678N1Z9
- **GST Rate**: 18% (CGST 9% + SGST 9%)
- **TDS**: Addressed (2% under Section 194C, 10% under 194J for consultancy)

## Budget Breakdown

1. **Civil Works & Infrastructure**: ₹4.5 Cr (36%)
2. **Tourism Facilities & Amenities**: ₹3 Cr (24%)
3. **Community Development & Training**: ₹2 Cr (16%)
4. **Equipment & Technology**: ₹1.5 Cr (12%)
5. **Project Management**: ₹1 Cr (8%)
6. **Contingency & Miscellaneous**: ₹0.5 Cr (4%)

**Total GST**: ₹2.25 Crores
**Grand Total**: ₹14.75 Crores

## Key Deliverables

1. 25 community-managed homestays (200 beds capacity)
2. 3 eco-lodges with traditional Monpa architecture (75 rooms)
3. 8 trekking trails (120 km total) with signage
4. Heritage Interpretation Center at Tawang Monastery
5. Skill training for 500 local youth
6. Waste management and solar installations
7. Tourism mobile app and booking portal

## Major Milestones

- **Month 1-4**: DPR finalization, clearances, land transfer, consultations
- **Month 5-12**: Civil construction, trail development Phase-I, infrastructure
- **Month 13-18**: Furnishing, interpretation center, training, trials
- **Month 19-24**: Marketing, portal launch, soft opening, handover

## Out of Scope

- Road connectivity improvements (PWD/BRO)
- Tawang Monastery renovation (heritage project)
- Airport/helipad construction (AAI)
- Maintenance after 3-year warranty
- Marketing beyond initial campaign
- Wildlife conservation (Forest Dept.)

## Payment Schedule

1. **40%** (₹5 Cr) - Upon contract signing + bank guarantee
2. **30%** (₹3.75 Cr) - At 50% physical progress
3. **20%** (₹2.5 Cr) - At 80% completion + preliminary acceptance
4. **10%** (₹1.25 Cr) - Final completion + 3-month defect liability

All payments subject to 2% TDS deduction.

## Environmental & Compliance Requirements

- **EIA**: Required under EIA Notification 2006 (>20,000 sq.m construction)
- **Forest Clearance**: FCA 1980 (12 hectares in deemed forest)
- **Wildlife Clearance**: State Wildlife Board (within 10km of Eaglenest)
- **Pollution Control**: State PCB approval for construction
- **Land Status**: 30 ha revenue land, 12 ha forest, 3 ha community (Monastery Trust)
